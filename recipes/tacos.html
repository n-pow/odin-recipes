Tacos

Description

These simple beef tacos can't be beaten when it comes to how much flavor you get from them in such a short time.

The best part is that everyone can customize them with whatever toppings they want to make their perfect taco.

Steps:

  1. Brown ground beef in skillet.
  2. While beef is cooking, prepare other tooppings you will use such as grated cheese, chopped lettuce, tomatoes, etc.
  3. Once beef is cooked, add taco seasoning and then half a cup of water. Stir to incorporate.
  4. Let reduce for 5-10 mins or until it has reached desired consistency.
  5. Warm your tortillas. Enjoy!
