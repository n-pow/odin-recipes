Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a baked pasta dish that consists of layers of noodles with sauce, cheese and often meat in between.

The result is a savory, filling meal that everyone is sure to enjoy.

Steps:

  1. Cook lasagna noodles til done.
  2. Get baking dish and add a thin layer of sauce to cover the bottom of pan.
  3. Add first layer of noodles. Spoon more sauce on top and then cover with a layer of cheese.
  4. Repeat until you have several layers built up.
  5. Put in oven for 30 min. Enjoy!
