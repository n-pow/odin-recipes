Easy Stir Fry

Description

Stir fry dishes are a great option for a quick weeknight meal that is packed with flavor and healthy vegetables.

They are highly customizable for your favorite protein & veggies, or just whatever you have around the house.

Steps:

  1. Prepare your rice or noodles that you will be using in the dish.
  2. Stir together a quick sauce made up of soy sauce, sesame oil, fish sauce, etc.
  3. Add your protein of choice to a hot wok with oil that is just about smoking.
  4. Remove from wok and add veggies in order of thickness.
  5. After a few minutes of cooking the veggies, add roughly half of the sauce and stir.
  6. Add your protein back into the wok, and your noodles if using them, and then add the remaining sauce.
  7. Enjoy!
